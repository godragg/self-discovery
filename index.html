The framework at a glance

🗓 When to use which reflection

Come back to this page whenever one of these triggers hits.

Annually or multi-year

Intrinsic Motivation Reflection

For setting or revisiting your long-term north star. Best done during a transition, a quiet season, or when something feels off.

  • New year / birthday
  • After a major life event
  • When you feel stuck or restless
  • Before a big career decision
Every 3 months or less

Short-term Needs Reflection

For your sprint cycle. Quick check-in on your current state — no need for big clarity, just honesty about right now.

  • Start of each quarter
  • After a tough week or big project
  • Whenever you feel flat or off
  • Revisit 4 weeks after each sprint
Reflect — understand yourself

🌱 Intrinsic Motivation Reflection

Hi! I'm doing a self-reflection exercise to better understand myself and what energises me intrinsically. Please help me find patterns and understand what they say about who I am and what work or life I'm drawn to. Here's what I notice: • If I didn't get paid, I would still want to do: [ your answer ] • I admire this about myself: [ your answer ], because [ why ] • I feel most energised when: [ your answer ] • I enjoy [ activity A ] more than [ activity B ] • The people I most admire are: [ describe them ], because [ what qualities ] • I love working with people who are: [ describe ] • The environments that energise me look like: [ describe ] Please: 1. Find patterns across these answers 2. Reflect back what you notice about what drives me intrinsically 3. Ask me 2–3 follow-up questions to go deeper I'll keep sharing more as we talk.

⚡ Short-term Needs Reflection

I'm not sure about my long-term direction yet — and that's okay. Can you help me with a shorter-term reflection instead? Right now, my state of mind feels like: [ e.g. restless / curious / flat / tired / overwhelmed ] If I have a magic wand, the first thing I'd want to change or achieve or do is: [ your answer ] What does this tell you about what I might be longing for right now?

Iterate — define your north star, backlog & sprint

🔄 Iteration Template

🌟 North Star

Your measurable desire in one metric, with a timeline if relevant.

Eg. "What measurable effect should I see if all backlog items are accomplished?"

📋 Backlog

Three skills or experiences you need to reach your north star.

⚡ Sprint

One small win you can ship this week. Be specific.

Not "workout" — try "15 min sweat + 15 min stretch on Tue & Thu"
